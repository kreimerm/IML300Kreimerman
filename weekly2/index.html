What is Californian Ideology?

The Californian ideology is that computers will liberate us from all the old forms of political control and we will become grandiose heroes in control of our own destiny.

How is Ayn Rand's philosophy relevant today?

This philosophy is relevant today because it's followed by many of the individuals that have the power, influence, and money to shape the world we live in. Following a set of values that justify prioritizing self-interest can be detrimental for the communties it impacts.
